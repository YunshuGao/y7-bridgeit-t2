L05 · Design Brief §1 Brief

§1. Design Brief

A short description of what you are making, who it's for, and why.

Problem / Need: "The challenge I am addressing is… because…"
Audience / Users: "The bridge is designed for… who need…"
Constraints: "The bridge must… while still…"
L05 · Specification §2 Specification

§2. Specification (checklist of rules)

A list of requirements your bridge must follow. Think: what must it DO, what must it be MADE FROM, what SIZE, what LIMITS?

Function: "The bridge must be able to…"
Materials: "It will be made from… (119 plain sticks + 16 holed sticks + clothes pegs + PVA)"
Size / Shape: "The bridge must fit… It should be no bigger than…"
Other Limits: "It cannot use more than… / must be completed in…"
