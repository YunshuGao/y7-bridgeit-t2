Bridge It 🌉

Design, build and load-test a popsicle-stick truss bridge. 22 lessons. Pair work. One big assessment. Everything you need is on this page.

📋 Your assessment — one task, three parts

Part A · Design Folio (40%)
Due end of Lesson 14 (Week 7).
Digital folio with brief, research, sketches, scaled + isometric drawings, materials list, WHS.
Part B · Bridge Build (30%)
Due Lesson 20 (Week 10) — Load Test Day.
Physical bridge + dated build journal with photos.
Part C · Evaluation (30%)
Due end of Lesson 22 (Week 11).
Load-test data + 400–600 word written evaluation.
