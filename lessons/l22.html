Week 11 · Lesson 22

Showcase + class awards + unit reflection

Week 11 · 75 minutes · Year 7 Technology (Mandatory) · Bridge It

TE4-1DPTE4-10TS

🎯 Learning intentions

  • I can present my bridge to peers and explain my design choices.
  • I can celebrate peers' work through the class awards.
  • I can reflect on what I've learned across the whole term.

✅ Success criteria

  • I have presented my bridge in a 1-minute gallery-walk pitch.
  • I have voted in at least 3 award categories.
  • My final evaluation is submitted to Google Classroom.
  • I have completed the unit reflection survey.

Do Now · 5 min

Set up your bridge (or the pieces of it!) on the bench with a printed card showing: team name, truss type, span, mass, max load, load-to-mass ratio. Tidy your bench.

I Do · ~10 min Teacher demonstration

Teacher introduces the award categories:

  • 🥇 Strongest Bridge — highest max load
  • 🪶 Lightest Bridge — lowest mass
  • 📊 Best Load-to-Mass Ratio — engineering efficiency award
  • 🎨 Most Beautiful — best aesthetics
  • 📖 Best Documentation — cleanest folio
  • 🔧 Best Recovery — team that improved most between dry-run and test
  • 🧠 Most Thoughtful Evaluation — sharpest Part C writing

Ms Gao announces load leaderboard and ratio leaderboard from L20 data.

We Do · ~15 min Guided practice

Gallery walk — 15 min. Every pair stands by their bridge. Class rotates. At each bridge, the building pair gives a 1-minute pitch covering:

  1. What type of truss is it?
  2. Max load?
  3. One thing you're proud of?
  4. One thing you'd do differently?

Voters jot top 3 picks in each category on a voting card.

You Do · ~35 min Independent pair work

Final submissions + reflection + awards (35 min).

  1. Submit final evaluation (Part C) to Google Classroom as PDF. Verify submission.
  2. Complete the unit reflection survey (Google Form).
  3. Vote in award categories.
  4. Ms Gao announces winners. Class applauds. (No physical prizes — just bragging rights and certificates.)
  5. Teacher preview of Term 3 — what's coming next.
You finished Bridge It. Your first summative assessment in high school Technology. Whatever your mark, you've built something that can hold your body weight. That's real.

Differentiation

🪜Support

Pitch with partner only; written reflection with sentence starters.

🎯Core

Individual pitch + full reflection survey.

🚀Extension

Present to a visiting teacher/HT. Write a short 'what I'd tell next year's Year 7' paragraph for the hub.

Exit Ticket · 5 min

  1. One skill I've built this term that I didn't have in January: ___
  2. Favourite moment of the term: ___
  3. Something I'd want to build next: ___
