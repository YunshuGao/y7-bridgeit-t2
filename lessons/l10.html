Week 5 · Lesson 10

Materials list, cost plan and WHS risk assessment

Week 5 · 75 minutes · Year 7 Technology (Mandatory) · Bridge It

TE4-2DPTE4-3DPTE4-9MA

🎯 Learning intentions

  • I can calculate how many popsicle sticks my design will use.
  • I can write a WHS risk assessment for scissors, glue gun, and load testing.
  • I can plan which parts of the build happen in which lesson (L11–L18).

✅ Success criteria

  • My folio has a materials list with stick count, glue quantity, and masking tape.
  • My folio has a WHS risk table with 4+ hazards, controls, and risk ratings.
  • My folio has a build schedule — which lesson does which stage.

Do Now · 5 min

Count the members on your plan + elevation drawing from L08. Quick estimate:

  • How many popsicle sticks for top + bottom chords? ___
  • How many for verticals? ___
  • How many for diagonals? ___
  • Double it (you have TWO trusses + deck): ___

I Do · ~10 min Teacher demonstration

Teacher models a materials list using the exemplar bridge:

MATERIALS LIST — Bridge It (Team Bradfield)
Popsicle sticks (full length 114 mm)     × 90
Popsicle sticks (cut to 60 mm)           × 24
Popsicle sticks (cut to 40 mm diagonals) × 32
PVA wood glue                            150 g
Hot glue sticks                          3 × 7 mm
Masking tape (temp clamping)             1 m
TOTAL STICKS                             ~146 (add 20% contingency = 175)

Then a WHS risk table. Teacher introduces the risk matrix: Likelihood × Consequence = Risk Level.

HAZARD                        LIKELIHOOD   CONSEQUENCE   RISK    CONTROL
Scissor cut                   Medium       Low           Low     Cut away from body, pass handle-first
Hot glue burn                 Medium       Medium        Med     Teacher-supervised only, cool-down mat
PVA in eyes                   Low          Low           Low     Wash hands, cap bottles
Bridge failure hits student   Low          Low           Low     1 m exclusion zone during load test

We Do · ~15 min Guided practice

Together we plan a build schedule:

  • L11 — WHS briefing + lay out + glue first base chord
  • L12 — complete base chord + cut verticals
  • L13 — glue diagonals (triangulation lesson)
  • L14 — mirror truss (second side)
  • L15 — deck
  • L16 — peer review + one improvement
  • L17 — reinforcement
  • L18 — finishing

You Do · ~35 min Independent pair work

Complete Part A of your folio (30 min).

  1. Write your MATERIALS LIST with quantities and total stick count.
  2. Write your WHS RISK TABLE — minimum 4 hazards, controls, risk levels before & after.
  3. Write your BUILD SCHEDULE — which build stage in which lesson.
  4. Check your whole folio against the Part A checklist (on hub). Tick each row.
  5. Save to Google Classroom draft folder — FORMATIVE CHECK-IN with Ms Gao (she'll mark up and return before Part A due date).
Your Part A folio is DUE at the end of Lesson 14 (Week 7). You have 4 more lessons to polish it — use L11–L14 build time to also polish folio evidence.

Differentiation

🪜Support

Partially pre-filled templates for materials list and WHS table. Teacher-checked build schedule provided.

🎯Core

Complete all three sections from scratch using templates.

🚀Extension

Add a cost-per-bridge calculation. Include contingency for broken members (estimate 15%). Add a 'what if?' row for one unexpected risk.

Exit Ticket · 5 min

  1. Total stick count for your bridge?
  2. Most serious WHS risk in your build?
  3. Which lesson is your build SCHEDULED to finish?
